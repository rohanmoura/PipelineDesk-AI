# PipelineDesk AI Case Study Draft

## Project Summary

PipelineDesk AI is an internal CRM and client operations dashboard for small agencies, consultants, and service teams. It helps teams manage client inquiries, qualify leads, move deals through a pipeline, track follow-ups, generate proposal briefs, hand off won deals to delivery, and monitor revenue insights from one focused workspace.

## Problem

Small service teams often manage sales and delivery across spreadsheets, email, DMs, chat threads, and documents. This creates missed follow-ups, unclear lead quality, scattered proposal status, weak revenue visibility, and messy handoffs after a deal is won.

## Solution

PipelineDesk AI turns scattered client operations into a clear workflow:

- Capture and qualify leads with AI-style scoring.
- Move deals through New Lead, Qualified, Proposal Sent, Negotiation, Won, and Lost stages.
- Track overdue, today, upcoming, and completed follow-ups.
- Generate realistic proposal briefs from lead details.
- Convert won deals into delivery handoff checklists.
- Monitor pipeline value, forecast, close rate, source performance, and owner performance.
- Configure team roles, scoring rules, pipeline stages, proposal templates, notifications, and CSV export settings.

## Key Features

- Professional dashboard overview with calculated pipeline metrics.
- Searchable and filterable leads table.
- Lead detail with owner assignment, notes, follow-up creation, and convert-to-deal action.
- Pipeline board with click-to-move stage actions and Lost logic.
- Follow-up center with scheduling, completion notes, and grouped task queues.
- Proposal AI workspace with loading, error, success, copy, retry, and export flows.
- Dynamic handoff page for won deals.
- Clients/accounts workspace with account detail and export.
- Analytics reports with filters, saved views, animated charts, labels, and no-data states.
- Settings page with role switching, permissions, workflow rules, notifications, CSV mock, and reset controls.
- Role-based behavior for Owner, Team member, and Viewer modes.

## Business Value

- Reduces missed follow-ups.
- Makes pipeline value and forecast visible.
- Helps teams identify strong and weak leads quickly.
- Keeps proposals, follow-ups, and handoffs connected.
- Gives owners a practical view of sales and operations performance.
